Why Blood Donation Matters

Eligibility

Anyone between 18-65 years, weighing 50kg or more, and in good health can donate blood. A simple screening ensures you're ready to help save lives.

Health Benefits

Regular blood donation helps maintain healthy iron levels, reduces cardiovascular risks, and provides free health screenings for donors.

Myths vs Facts

Donating blood is safe, doesn't weaken your immune system, and your body regenerates the donated blood within 24-48 hours.
